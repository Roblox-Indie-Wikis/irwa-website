Joining

All new member wikis are expected to meet the following list of criteria. Please note that achieving all of these criteria does not guarantee membership. For certain criteria, we willing to accomodate and make exceptions for certain criteria and will evaluate this on a case-by-case basis. 

Wiki Subject
Your wiki's content must be centered around Roblox, whether it be through documenting a Roblox game, game franchise, development studio, etc. The content that your wiki covers must be compliant with Roblox's Community Guidelines and be appropriate for young audiences, as well as all content on your wiki. We reserve the right to refuse any wikis that we believe do not comply with these standards.

Activity
Your wiki should maintain an active wiki community and editorbase proportional to the popularity of your wiki's subject game. This includes keeping content up to date and monitoring for spam and vandalism. Wikis covering inactive subject games may not need to be edited as frequently to be considered active. 

independence
Your wiki should be independent, meaning that the power for decisions and control should rest mainly with the wiki's staff team and not any external groups (hosting providers, wiki farms, etc). This includes decisions related to wiki management, styling, and technical changes. Wikis hosted on Fandom are not permitted to join IRWA.

Status
Your wiki must either be officially recongised or endorsed by the developers, or the de-facto community official wiki of the community. If there is another wiki that is not from an official fork or migration, the wiki submitted should be of significantly higher quality as well as having one of the three listed attributes above. We do not include forked wikis when there is an official, endorsed one.
